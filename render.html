Our Services

Texture & Granosite Coating

Does your building have some surface defects you wish to hide? Surface defects can make a structure appear less-than-professional. Our texture and granosite coating services provide the perfect solution to enhance your building's appearance.

Acrylic Rendering Sydney

Looking for rendering services in Sydney? Acrylic render is a great investment if you have a home with exterior walls that need protection and beautification. Our professional acrylic rendering delivers outstanding results.

Cement Rendering Sydney

Cement render is a massive, thick, and immovable covering over a building's substrate. It was first used in Europe hundreds of years ago and remains one of the most durable rendering solutions available today.

Cement Rendering Repairs

ProRender tends to large and small cement rendering repairs. We offer affordable prices, professional work with years of experience. Trust us to restore your rendering to its original condition.

Concrete Rendering Sydney
